### 1. What is AI?
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

### 2. What is Machine Learning?
Machine Learning enables computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.

### 3. What is Deep Learning?
Deep Learning uses neural networks with many layers to learn patterns from large datasets.

### 4. What are Large Language Models (LLMs)?
LLMs are AI models trained on huge text data to generate and understand human-like language.
